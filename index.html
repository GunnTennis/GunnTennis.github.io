SUMMER CAMP

Gunn High School

Welcome back to Summer Tennis Camp, where safety is our top priority! We're committed to following CDC guidelines for tennis, ensuring a secure environment for your kids. As part of our measures, players will need to disinfect their hands upon arrival and departure, adhere to social distancing norms, and we'll limit each court to 6-8 players. Additionally, our team will diligently disinfect equipment after each session. Our program caters to all grade levels, from 1st to 12th, offering a blend of on and off-court activities. Join us for engaging drills, live ball skills, and exciting games. Beyond the physical benefits, we aim to foster confidence, coordination, and most importantly, a love for the lifelong sport of tennis. Let's get your kids out of the house, onto the court, and having a blast while staying active!
